# Privacy Policy

MakeSoil ("MakeSoil," "we," "us") provides a platform that connects people to compost together. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when you use the MakeSoil website or mobile app. If you interact with us on a third-party platform (for example, a social media service), that platform's own privacy policy governs how it handles your information.

We don't sell your personal information, and we don't share it with third parties except as described below.

By using MakeSoil, you agree to this policy and our [Terms of Use](https://www.makesoil.org/terms).

## Information We Collect

We collect information in a few broad categories:

- **Information you provide.** Anything you enter into the service — when creating an account, setting up or joining a soil site, posting content, sending messages, contacting us, or responding to optional prompts or surveys. This can include identifiers (name, email, phone), profile details, locations, photos, and the content of your messages and posts.
- **Automatically collected information.** We collect standard technical information your browser or device sends when using the service, such as IP address, browser type, timestamps, referring pages, device information, and server log data. We use essential cookies and similar storage as needed to operate the Service. We do not use third-party analytics tools, advertising cookies, or other non-essential tracking cookies.
- **Payment information.** MakeSoil does not collect or store payment-card details. Payments, donations, and paid soil-site access may be handled by third-party payment processors under their own terms. We may receive and store related transaction or subscription records needed to operate the Service.

## How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to operate the platform, authenticate users, facilitate connections between soil site hosts (Soil Makers) and their participants (soil supporters), respond to inquiries, send service-related messages, send community or platform updates, prevent abuse, and improve the service.

## When We Share Information

We share information only in these limited cases:

- **Service providers** who help us run the platform — hosting, storage, maps, spam prevention, payments, email delivery, error monitoring, customer support, and similar functions — and only to the extent needed to do that work.
- **Legal requirements**, in good faith, to comply with law, respond to lawful requests, protect the rights or safety of MakeSoil or its users, or investigate possible violations of our Terms.
- **Continuity events.** If MakeSoil undergoes a merger, acquisition, restructuring, dissolution, reorganization, bankruptcy, asset sale, or transfer of substantially all assets — including a transition between nonprofit, public benefit, cooperative, or for-profit organizational structures undertaken to ensure the long-term sustainability of the platform and mission — user information, accounts, and platform operations may be transferred to a successor entity that continues substantially similar services or mission-aligned operations. Any successor entity will be expected to honor the material privacy commitments described in this policy, or to give users notice of material changes. User information will not be sold to third parties for unrelated advertising or data-broker purposes. In the event of a material change of ownership or control affecting the platform, users may be notified and given an opportunity to delete their account where reasonably practicable.

## Content You Post and Visibility

Some of what you share is visible to other users by design. Soil-site listings, including their locations, may appear on the public map, may be approximate, or may be shared with other users so the platform can do its job. Full address and access instructions for a soil site may be shared with participants when needed to operate that soil site. When you join or ask to join a soil site, your name, profile photo, role, request message, participation status, and activity in that soil site may be visible to the Soil Maker and/or other participants. Posts, profile details, and messages may be visible to the users they're directed to or to the broader community. Once shared, content may be seen, copied, or redistributed by others — use your judgment about what you share.

## Cookies

We use cookies and similar storage to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and secure the Service. If you configure your browser to refuse cookies, some parts of the Service may not work properly.

## Your Choices

- **Update your information.** You can review and update most of your account information at any time by signing in and editing your profile or soil-site settings.
- **Delete your account.** You can delete your account through your account settings, which will delete or de-identify your account information from the active service, except where we need to keep limited records for legal, security, or operational reasons. If you can't reach that option, you can contact us at the link or email below.
- **Informational and system-event emails.** Unsubscribe either by the link in the email (if present) or by updating your account settings. We may still send necessary service messages (such as updates to this policy).

## Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your information, but no online service can guarantee absolute security. Please choose a strong password, keep it confidential, and log out after using shared devices.

## International Users

MakeSoil is operated from the United States. If you access the service from outside the U.S., you understand that your information will be processed in the U.S., which may have different data-protection laws than your country.

## Links to Other Sites

The platform may link to third-party sites or services. We aren't responsible for their privacy practices — review their policies before using them.

## Children

MakeSoil is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please email us and we will investigate and remove information we identify as collected from a child under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we'll notify registered users by email or by posting a prominent notice on the site before the changes take effect. Continued use of the platform after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
